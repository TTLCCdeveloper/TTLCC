Medical Consumables Start-up

Mushrad

Acoustically perfect

Mushrad is a start-up company under Technology Transfer, Licensing and Commercialisation Centre which is dedicated to producing a superior ultrasound gel that revolutionizes healthcare diagnostics and treatments. Our gel is designed to be a game-changer in various medical applications, including diagnostic ultrasound scanning, physiotherapy treatments, Doppler assessments, and fetal heart rate monitoring in antenatal clinics. With a strong focus on innovation, Mushrad aims to deliver an acoustically perfect and high-quality ultrasound gel, setting a new standard in the industry and driving growth across all aspects of our business.

Lithium battery

EMRECC

Renewable energy for all

EMRECC has made significant progress in the production of battery grade lithium cathode from Zimbabwe based lithium deposits which was then used to make lithium cells prototype for possible applications in Energy Storage Systems and Electro Mobility. This breakthrough has the potential to accelerate the country’s involvement in clean energy technologies. Zimbabwe boasts one of the world’s largest reserves of hard-rock lithium, a crucial mineral used in the production of lithium-ion batteries. EMRECC has successfully purified lithium phosphate and manufactured prototype cells, marking a significant step towards commercial production.
